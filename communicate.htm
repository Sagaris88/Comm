About Me

As a recent graduate of the University of Toronto with High Distinction, I have theoretical experience but now am looking for practical experience in the real world where I can use and hone my skills. My combination of Majors in Visual Studies and Architectural Design enables me to have dual skill sets involving creativity and technology. Aside from my academic streams, I have a special interest in urban planning and transit planning. Using my creative and technological skills, I have dabbled in making fantasy transit maps to portray a future to what different cities' transit networks should be. These maps have gained my notoriety online and even attracted the attention of CBC Radio. While many design practices nowadays revolve around the digital, I love being hands-on as I have been handling mechanical tools and fixing problems since high school.
